Welcome to the Bend Rock Gym

Bend Rock Gym offers a 3-in-1 climbing gym experience by combining 3 unique gyms into one location.
--Our North Gym features our biggest walls for sport climbing, as well as our front desk, yoga studio, and workout area.
--In the West Gym, you'll find an 11,000 sq ft bouldering gym.
--The South Gym is our original building, and features a mix of bouldering and sport climbing, including 17 auto-belays.
No matter the gym, you'll find a friendly, supportive, safety-oriented staff to help you have fun and stay safe.
Whether you're an experienced mountaineer chasing a sponsorship, or you're looking to rope-up for the first time, you'll find BRG has what you need. We offer a variety of classes, rentals, and other services to help you learn, improve, and have fun safely.
